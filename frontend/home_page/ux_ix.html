Designer UI/UX

Read More

Key Responsibilities

  • Scale visual solutions within interfaces.
  • Collaborate with designers and artists to develop and launch new projects in the gaming industry.
  • Generate creative ideas to visualize concepts and promote products/services.
  • Ensure consistent brand visual style across all marketing materials.
  • Work with components and design systems in Figma.
  • Collaborate closely with front-end developers and design teams.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 5+ years of professional design experience.
  • Excellent sense of style — ability to harmoniously combine colors, fonts, and compositions.
  • Strong attention to visual detail and precision.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team and meet deadlines.
  • Genuine passion for gaming projects.
  • Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and responsive web design principles.
  • Experience designing complex systems.
  • Solid understanding of UI/UX principles, design guidelines, responsive design, Mobile First, and Design Thinking.
  • Proficiency with design systems, component libraries, and style guides.
  • Experience with graphic editing and retouching.
