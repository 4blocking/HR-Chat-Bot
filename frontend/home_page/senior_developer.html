Senior Developer

Read More

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and optimize robust solutions to enhance the proprietary payments platform, ensuring high performance, scalability, and security.
  • Develop and maintain complex integrations with payment providers, banks, and third-party systems, ensuring seamless data flow and alignment with technical and regulatory requirements.
  • Actively participate in all stages of new feature development — from initial concept and technical design to implementation and delivery.
  • Contribute to the evolution of the fintech payments solution, ensuring innovation and adaptability to market needs.
  • Collaborate within agile development cycles.
  • Identify opportunities for technical and process improvements, driving efficiency and innovation throughout the software lifecycle.
  • Work closely with design, product, and business teams to ensure features meet user expectations and business objectives.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 5+ years of commercial experience in web development.
  • 3+ years of hands-on experience with Node.js.
  • 3+ years of frontend development experience using React.
  • Solid experience with NextJS, NestJS, Apollo, and Material UI.
  • Strong proficiency in TypeScript.
  • Commercial experience working with high-load SQL or NoSQL databases.
  • Familiarity with REST, GraphQL, and WebSockets.
  • Understanding of TDD and writing effective automated tests.
  • Experience with microservice architecture principles.
  • Proficient with Git version control workflows.
  • Hands-on experience with RabbitMQ and Redis.
  • Experience with MongoDB and MySQL.
  • Advanced English proficiency (written and spoken).
